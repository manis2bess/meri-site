Search experience

I work in the search team during convulsive times. The teams' merge, technical legacy to clean up, a profound change of all the components of the team and new disciplines to learn to collaborate with. This unique times, gave me the opportunity to do what it wasn’t possible: presenting adapted search result pages based on predicted customer intents.

SKILLS

COMPANY

IKEA 2023

CREDITS

Listing team (15 coworkers)

The goal

The application presented a "one-fit-all” journey, based on the standard product-type of search in e-commerce sites. It presented several inefficiencies when encountering multiple search terms or queries which weren't necessary products and accesibility issues.

Process

The process naturally followed an HCD approach from empathising with the users that helped to create a “model” or hypothesis, set the systems to be able to run analytics, and phrase the right problem in the definition phase. I involved the team from the definition phase. It helped not only to confirm the model and share the same goal across the team, but also engineering and data scientist were able to come with a plan to train the algorithm to do want we wanted it to do.

I used different techniques: analytics, desk research, usabillity tests and qualitative researches.

I destiled the learnings in a "model" to based the future work.

I designed the team's tracking tool that will allow as to map behaviours.
